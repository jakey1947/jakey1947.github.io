About me

I'm a game developer from brisbane skilled in all things programming and 3D art. I've always been a gamer since I was little, growing up I sunk hours upon hours into the PS2 playing Ratchet and Clank, the Jak series and eventually switch to PC playing games like Titan Quest and whatever flash game I could get my hands on.

I'm primarily a programmer but I also do a lot of 3D art. My primary languages are C# and Rust and for 3D I use blender and geometry nodes.

Games

Stygian Descent
Single Player Roguelike Platformer with melee focused combat and dozens of items
I was the lead programmer on this project and created the frameworks for items, enemy AI and level generation for the game designers to use.
Cult of Azathoth
Single Player Roguelike Idle game where you run a cult and must balance resources to capture and sacrifice in the name of keeping Azathoth alseep.
Created over the course of a week between assignments with a small team of freshmen at QUT for the QUT Winter Game Jam.
Lego Masters Prototype
Singleplayer VR Lego Masters game where you race to build structures from provided prompts, made in partnership with Banijay Entertainment as a University Project at QUT.

I was the lead programmer on this project and implemented the mesh generation, input, gameplay loop, game design and all the backend for those systems. This game is not publicly available because of NDA.

Firebending VR Prototype
A prototype for a 2 player online arena fighting game where the player can use powers similar to that of Avatar's fire and lightning bending. Created over the course of 2 weeks this project's purpose was both an assignment, and to learn the ins and outs of VR game development.
Witch Tales
4 Player tron like party game where you must defeat the other players by blocking them with your trail.
I did the majority of the programming as well as look dev for this project.
